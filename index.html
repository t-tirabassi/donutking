Donut King

PHONE: 808-258-4119

WEBSITE: donutkinghawaii.com

Monday-Sunday: 6:00 AM – 5:30 PM

DINE-IN: No TAKEOUT: Yes

Since opening at Market City, Donut King has become the happy place for donut lovers. The glass display cases feature delectable rounds of doughy goodness, everything from Old Fashion, Chocolate Rainbow Sprinkle Cake, Honey Glazed Twist, Red Velvet Cake, Honey Glazed Crumbed, and Apple Fritter, to donut holes, sure to satisfy any sweet cravings. Fan favorites: sweet and savory Maple Bacon and the equally addicting glazed Croissant Bites.
